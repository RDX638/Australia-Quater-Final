Melbourne

Melbourne, city, capital of the state of Victoria, Australia. It is located at the head of Port Phillip Bay, on the southeastern coast.

Perth

Perth City Centre is located alongside the peaceful waters of the Swan River, twenty Kilometres inland of the Indian Ocean on the West Coast of Australia.

Byron Bay

Byron Bay is a great place to get active, be it surfing, kayaking, fishing, sailing or just enjoying a swim at a beautiful beach.

Explore Australia

As a nonprofit organization, at Visit Australia, our mission is to develop and maintain marketing programs – in partnership with the state’s travel industry – that inspire travel to, and within, Australia. We work hard to promote and market Australia as a premier tourist destination. From the mountains to the beaches, the redwoods to the deserts and from our metropolitan cities to our unique small towns, we see it as our job to inspire travel to this amazing state, one visitor at a time. We’re excited that you’re considering a vacation and glad you visited our website to begin planning. We hope you find your inspiration, information and the links to help make your vacation a reality.
